More youth.

Heart in Motion connects social good organizations and schools with youth volunteers they need to carry out their critical work. Collectively, our youth have provided 111,376 community service hours.
 
Since 2010, we've facilitated nearly 21,505 nonprofit volunteer connections for Bay Area youth. But there's more to be done.
 
As a 501(c)3 nonprofit ourselves, we rely on donations to carry out our mission to strengthens communities through promoting volunteerism and leadership development in youth.
